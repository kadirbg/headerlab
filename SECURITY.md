# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in HeaderLab, please email **hello@headerlab.dev** with details. Do **not** open a public issue for security disclosures.

We aim to respond within 72 hours and work with you to address the issue before any public disclosure.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- The HeaderLab web application at headerlab.dev
- The analysis engines in `src/lib/`
- The Cloudflare Worker API at `/api/check`

**Out of scope:**

- Third-party services (Cloudflare, GitHub)
- Social engineering or physical attacks
- Denial-of-service / volumetric attacks
- Vulnerabilities requiring physical access to the user's device

## What we promise

- Acknowledge your report within 72 hours
- Provide a tentative timeline for the fix
- Credit you in the changelog (or anonymously, your choice)
- Not pursue legal action against good-faith research within scope
